Phonebloks is an independent organisation with the purpose of encouraging the development and production of products that produce less electronic waste.

Starting with your phone.
 

Phonebloks

Phonebloks all started with one video, an idea for a phone worth keeping. With a social media reach of over 380,000,000 people it has been quite succesful. We never intended to build a mobile phone ourselves. But it was an effective way to show the mobile industry the need for change.
Now we are trying to steer the industry towards a more modular direction, using the power of the people.
